GLOBAL WAR IMPACT ANALYSIS - KEY INSIGHTS

1. Total Wars Per Country
The top 10 countries by war participation show that conflict involvement is concentrated among a small group of countries.

2. Total Deaths Per Country
The highest total death counts are not always found in the countries with the most wars, showing that frequency and impact are different.

3. Average Deaths Per War
Some countries have fewer wars but much higher average deaths per conflict, indicating greater war intensity.

4. Total Wars By Region
War frequency is regionally concentrated, with a few regions accounting for most recorded conflicts.

5. Total Deaths By Region
The regions with the highest casualties highlight where war impact has been most severe in human terms.

6. GDP Vs War Frequency
Economic strength does not remove war involvement, but it helps reveal how war patterns differ across countries.

7. Deaths Per Million
Population-normalized results show which countries suffered the greatest relative damage, not just the largest raw death counts.

8. Global War Impact Summary
The combined analysis of war frequency, deaths, GDP, and population shows that smaller and economically weaker countries often face disproportionately higher impact.
